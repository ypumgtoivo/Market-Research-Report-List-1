<p><p>Off-grid solar PV panels are categorized into three main types: thin film, crystalline silicon, and others. </p><p>Thin film panels are lightweight and flexible, suitable for diverse applications but generally less efficient compared to crystalline options. Crystalline silicon panels, including monocrystalline and polycrystalline, offer higher efficiency and longer lifespans, making them the most popular choice. Other types may include emerging technologies like organic photovoltaics and concentrated solar power. Each type serves specific energy needs and installation scenarios in off-grid contexts.</p></p>
