<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-smoke Halogen-free</li><li>Low-smoke Low-Halogen</li><li>Low-smoke Halogen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame retardant cable market features three main types. Low-smoke halogen-free cables emit minimal smoke and are free of halogens, making them safe for environments where air quality is vital, such as public buildings. Low-smoke low-halogen cables reduce harmful emissions during combustion while still containing some halogens, balancing safety and performance. Low-smoke halogen cables, while providing good fire resistance, produce more smoke and toxic gases, limiting their use in sensitive applications. Each type serves specific safety and environmental needs.</p></p>
