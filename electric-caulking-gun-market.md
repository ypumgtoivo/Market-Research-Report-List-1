<p><p>The electric caulking gun market is primarily divided into two main types: closed barrel and open barrel caulking guns. Closed barrel caulking guns are designed with a cartridge that is sealed, allowing for a cleaner application and reducing the risk of clogs. In contrast, open barrel caulking guns hold bulk caulk, making them suitable for larger projects requiring more material and frequent refills. Both types enhance efficiency and ease of use, catering to various user needs in construction and renovation.</p></p>
