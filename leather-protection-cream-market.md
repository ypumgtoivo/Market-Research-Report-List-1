<p><strong>The Leather Protection Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solvent-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leather protection cream market can be categorized into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based creams are formulated with water as the primary solvent, offering a lightweight application that dries quickly and provides breathability. They are often favored for their eco-friendly properties and ease of cleaning. Solvent-based creams, in contrast, use organic solvents, providing a more substantial protective layer and enhanced water resistance. These creams are typically more durable but may require caution due to potential environmental impacts and odor.</p></p>
