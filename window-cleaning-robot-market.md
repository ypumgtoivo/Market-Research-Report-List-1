<p><strong>The Window Cleaning Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The window cleaning robot market comprises two primary types: vacuum suction and fan adsorption. Vacuum suction models use powerful suction mechanisms to adhere to glass surfaces, effectively lifting and removing dirt and grime. In contrast, fan adsorption robots utilize high-velocity fans to create a pressure differential, allowing them to cling to windows while cleaning. Both technologies aim to enhance window cleaning efficiency, but they differ in their approach to adhering to surfaces and cleaning effectiveness.</p></p>
