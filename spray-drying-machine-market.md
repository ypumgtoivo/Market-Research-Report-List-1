<p><p>The spray drying machine market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Pressure Spray Dryer**: Utilizes high pressure to atomize the feed, producing fine droplets for rapid drying. </p><p>2. **Stream Spray Dryer**: Employs a controlled airflow to optimize the drying process, enhancing efficiency and product quality. </p><p>3. **Centrifuging Spray Dryer**: Uses centrifugal force to disperse the liquid feed, ensuring uniform droplet size and consistent drying results. Each type caters to specific applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and chemical industries.</p></p>
