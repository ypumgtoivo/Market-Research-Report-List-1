<p><ul><li>Amplifier</li><li>Attenuator</li><li>Diode</li><li>Driver</li><li>Frequency Multiplier</li><li>Limiter Diode</li><li>Mixer</li><li>Read/Write</li><li>Receiver</li><li>Smart Card IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF die products encompass a range of components crucial for signal processing in wireless communications. Amplifiers boost signal strength, while attenuators reduce signal amplitude. Diodes offer rectification and signal mixing capabilities, and drivers provide power to transmit signals effectively. Frequency multipliers generate higher frequency signals, while limiter diodes protect circuits from excessive power. Mixers combine different frequencies, and read/write ICs facilitate data transfer. Receivers capture and decode signals, while smart card ICs enable secure communication in applications like payment systems.</p></p>
