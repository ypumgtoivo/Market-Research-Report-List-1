<p><p>The Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) power cable market includes two primary types: polypropylene and ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) cables. Polypropylene cables are known for their lightweight and resistance to chemical damage, making them suitable for varied environments. In contrast, EPDM cables offer enhanced flexibility and durability, particularly in high-temperature applications. Both types are designed to ensure reliable electrical connections for submersible pumps in oil and gas extraction, water supply, and industrial processes, addressing specific environmental and operational challenges.</p></p>
