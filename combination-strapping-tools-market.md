<p><p>The combination strapping tools market consists of three primary types: tensioners, sealers, and cutters. Tensioners are used to apply the necessary tension to strapping materials, securing packages tightly. Sealers work by joining the ends of the strapping together, ensuring that the packages remain intact during transport. Cutters are essential for trimming excess strapping after securing a package. Together, these tools enhance packaging efficiency and security in various industries, supporting effective supply chain management and logistics operations.</p></p>
